Zachary Lutes

A Brief History

Origin

In the years immediately following his birth on a moving circus train, Z. Lutes proved himself a keen example of a textbook ragamuffin. Early, there were some hard, dark years of membership in a band of orphaned but operatic pickpockets, singing throatily as they minced through crowded train stations and marketplaces, knicking trinkets, hastily returned to Fagin.
